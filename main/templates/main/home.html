About Us

YOLAD GLOBAL SERVICES LIMITED positions itself as a Need-Market Platform that offers skilled Workers, Personnels, Businesses and individual Clients a uniform and trouble-free way to connect in order to seamlessly exchange Values.

The company offers a business-to-business solution to the enormous request for Services as demanding as Housekeeping, Technical, Artistic and Support Work...
With our hands-on response to demands, we ensure an enabling Environment that dignifies Effort and Efficiency.

About The Platform

The ultimate purpose of this platform is to enhance ease of access, seamless connections and relationship that works between skilled workers and service users, with well-tailored transactions medium and a second-to-nontrusted and reliable deliverable system of service. The proposed project intends to expand the Need-market, thus causing an increase in a million of jobs creation. Due to lack of current competition, yoladglobal.com has the first mover advantage and is well positioned to establish itself as the leader in this newly created service category.
