Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a skilled service provider on Skillas?

Skillas offers a search feature that allows you to find service providers based on specific skills, location and reviews. Simply enter your requirements and our platform will match you with suitable providers.

What measures does Skillas take to ensure the reliability of service providers?

We carefully vet and verify service providers on our platform, checking their credentials and reviews from other users. This helps ensure the quality and reliability of the services offered.

How can I communicate with a service provider before hiring them?

Skillas provide a secure messaging system within the platform that allows users to communicate with service providers. This ensures easy and transparent communication throughout the hiring process.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the services provided?

Skillas has a resolution system in place to address any dissatisfaction. You can report this issue and our team will work to find a fair resolution, including possible refunds or finding an alternative service provider.

Is my payment information secure on Skillas?

Yes, Skillas takes the security of your payment information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your financial data.

How can I create compelling profile on Skillas?

To create an appealing profile, include details about your skills, experience and any certifications. Upload high-quality photos of your work and encourage satisfied clients to leave reviews.
